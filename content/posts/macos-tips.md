Here are some macOS tips I use every day:

## Shortcuts

- **Cmd+Space** - Spotlight search
- **Cmd+Tab** - App switcher
- **Cmd+`** - Same app window switch

## Finder

- **Cmd+Shift+.** - Show hidden files
- **Cmd+Option+V** - Move instead of copy

These small tips add up over time.
