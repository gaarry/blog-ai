Tokyo is an incredible city. Here are my notes:

## Neighborhoods

- **Shibuya** - Energy and crowds
- **Shinjuku** - Mix of old and new
- **Asakusa** - Traditional Japan
- **Harajuku** - Fashion and culture

## Tips

- Get a Suica card for transport
- Learn basic Japanese phrases
- Try the street food

Tokyo rewards exploration.
