I've been blogging on and off for years. Here's why I keep coming back:

## Reasons to Blog

1. **Documenting** - Forces me to write down what I learn
2. **Sharing** - Knowledge should be free
3. **Practice** - Writing improves with practice

## The Challenge

The hardest part is consistency. It's easy to start, hard to maintain.

But every post I write is a small victory. And over time, they add up.
